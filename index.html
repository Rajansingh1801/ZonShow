How it works

Create your Zone

The user for zonshow creates a zone using their phone. The zone data is owned by the user and stored on their device locally and is encrypted and uploaded to picParc servers to be managed by the user.

Managed by Size

TWhen creating the zone on zonshow App the Local zone data can also be locally managed by chosen the size, shape and time frame for an active zone. This can be by voice command or by selecting icons on the device screen.

Parking zones

There will be various types of zones to select, a parking zone to lease out, or a security parking zone that has a time limit and is turned off once the vehicle is moved from the temporary zone
